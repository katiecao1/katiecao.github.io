ABOUT

With 2 years of experience in analytics and finance at an economic consulting firm and public accounting firms, my goals are to increase revenue and efficiencies in the businesses that I work on. I intentionally built my skillset for results.

When I'm not coding or creating pretty charts, I enjoy baking, people-watching at the park, and hiking in Upstate New York. Big fan of the TV show The Office, The Great British Baking Show, and the Oxford comma.
